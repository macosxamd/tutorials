3. Fill in the SRV Record Details
Field	Value
Service	_vlmcs
Protocol	_tcp
Port number	1688
Host offering this service	kms.yourdomain.local (or the FQDN of your KMS host)

Click OK, then Done.

4. (Optional) Verify the Record

From any domain-joined client, run:

nslookup -type=srv _vlmcs._tcp.yourdomain.local


You should see your KMS host listed.

5. Confirm KMS Service Operation

On the KMS host, make sure the Software Protection Service is running:

net start sppsvc


Then check that port 1688 is listening:

netstat -ano | find "1688"
